Our team

A multidisciplinary group of nurses, scientists, and students working toward diabetes health equity.

Leadership

DT
Dante Anthony Tolentino, PhD, MS, RN-BC
Director & Principal Investigator

Filipino Americans represent one of the largest Asian American subgroups in the U.S., yet remain understudied. Dr. Tolentino studies type 2 diabetes health inequities among Filipino Americans through socio-cultural research and digital health interventions.
